NQIS Community

We strive to enable digital security among individuals, institutions and businesses of North Queensland. We believe that is made of inclusive, helpful and smart people. In general, we aim to share our expertise and experience to benefit all.
Our hope is to enable everyone to choose digital security, privacy, great software and a great community. If that interests you, we'd love to hear from you.

Support

If you need support from NQIS, please don't hesitate to ask, we're really happy to help. We have a chat room, a collaboration with the local university and social media.

Twitter

We have an official account (@nqis_) and a hashtag: #nqis.

Mailing List
